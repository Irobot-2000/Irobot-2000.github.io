Orbit Send

This is a website that makes sharing of files over a local area network very easy.

Framework 7 is the frontend framework while data transferring in handled by the simplepeer library. A wrapper aroud the webRTC api.

It allows sharing of text and access to camera too.

Taking group selfies has never been easier.

Try it out today at https://orbit-send.pages.dev .
